WHAT IS INTERIOR DESIGN?

Interior design goes beyond mere decoration; it is about understanding the needs and desires of individuals and translating them into tangible spaces that enhance their lives. Designers carefully consider factors such as the purpose of the space, the flow of movement, and the interaction between people and their surroundings. Aesthetics play a vital role in interior design, as it is the visual appeal that captivates and evokes emotions. Colors, textures, patterns, and materials are selected with care, aiming to create a desired ambiance and convey a specific mood or style. Whether it's a serene and minimalist design or a vibrant and eclectic one, the choices made in terms of aesthetics greatly influence the overall atmosphere of a space.
